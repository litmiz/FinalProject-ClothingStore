OUR RETURNS POLICY

We are sorry if you are not 100% satisfied with the items you received, and we will accept returns and replacements within 60 days from the purchase date.

Return Conditions

  • 1. If you receive damaged/defective/wrong items, please contact SHEIN Customer Service for help as soon as possible.
  • 2. Items returned must be unused, with the original packing. We do not accept returns on items that have been worn, damaged, washed or altered in any way.
  • 3. The following items cannot be returned or exchanged: bodysuits, lingerie and swimwear.
  • 4. We do not accept returned items that have been sent back without proper returns requests.
  • 5. We do not offer Freight to Collect (FTC) service for the packages returned to us. The returns will be made at your own cost.
  • 6. Please be sure to double check your returns before shipping them out. We will not be held responsible for the return of non-DRESS@HOME items.

Refunds

Your refund will be issued to the original payment account. Once your refund has been issued, you will receive a confirmation email.
